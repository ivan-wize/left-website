Income that knows when you're paid.

Set up your paycheck once — weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, or monthly — and Left. generates the expected entries for every future month. When payday arrives, tap to confirm. If the amount was different, adjust it in one motion. The ring updates instantly.

Add expenses in seconds.

Smart suggestions for your usual purchases. Auto-detected categories. Three taps and done.

Bills you check off.

Recurring monthly bills with one-tap "mark paid." Copy last month's bills forward in a single tap.

Save toward what matters.

Set a goal with a monthly target. Log contributions. Hit milestones with a small celebration, because they deserve one.

Plan the whole month.

Navigate forward and back through months. See what your ring looks like in April when you're still in February.

Everywhere you are

Left. lives on every Apple device you own.

Your budget shouldn't require a detour. Log an expense from your watch. Scan a receipt with your camera. Check what's left from your home screen without opening the app.

Scan receipts, instantly.

Point your camera at a receipt. Left. reads the merchant, total, and date. You just confirm the category. The image stays with the transaction, synced via iCloud.
