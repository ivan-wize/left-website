Frequently asked

Is Left. really free?

Yes. The core experience — tracking income, bills, expenses, savings goals, and receipts; seeing what's left to spend; navigating months — is completely free, with no ads and no data collection. Left. Pro is an optional, one-time purchase that unlocks advanced planning features (debt payoff strategies, net worth tracking, asset management). Pro starts at $5 and is yours forever, on all your devices.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no email, no password. Open the app and start using it. Your data syncs through your personal iCloud account automatically, and we have no account system of our own — by design.

Does Left. connect to my bank?

No, and we have no plans to. Bank linking requires a third-party aggregator (like Plaid), which introduces privacy trade-offs and recurring costs we don't want to pass on to you. Left. is manual-entry or receipt scanning, by design. If automatic import is critical for you, there are other excellent apps that offer it — we're not trying to be those apps.

How does iCloud sync work?

Left. uses Apple's CloudKit service, the same infrastructure your Photos, Notes, and Reminders use. As long as you're signed into iCloud on your iPhone and have iCloud Drive enabled, your data syncs automatically between devices running Left. (including Apple Watch). There's nothing to configure and nothing for us to see — Apple handles the encryption and sync end to end.

What happens if I delete and reinstall the app?

Your data comes back. Because everything is stored in your iCloud account rather than only on your device, reinstalling Left. and signing into the same iCloud account restores everything — income sources, bills, expenses, savings goals, receipts, the whole thing. You don't need to back anything up manually.

I bought Pro on another device. How do I restore it?

Open Left., go to the Pro upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchase at the bottom. As long as you're signed into the same Apple Account that made the original purchase, Pro unlocks immediately. This is Apple's standard purchase restoration and works on all Apple devices.

How does receipt scanning work?

Tap the camera icon in the quick-add bar on the Overview screen. Point your camera at a receipt, and Left. reads the merchant name, total, and date automatically using Apple's on-device Vision framework. The image never leaves your phone during scanning — it's all local. You just confirm the category and it's saved.

How do I add a Home Screen or Lock Screen widget?

Long-press your Home Screen or Lock Screen, tap the + button in the top-left, search for "Left.", and choose a size. Small, medium, and Lock Screen widgets are all available. They refresh automatically as you log transactions — no manual refresh needed.

Does Left. work on Apple Watch?

Yes. The Apple Watch companion app shows your daily budget, lets you mark bills as paid, and accepts quick expense logging from your wrist. It syncs with your iPhone through iCloud — there's no pairing step. Install the iPhone app and the Watch app appears automatically on your wrist (assuming your Watch is set up with your iPhone).

Can I export my data?

Yes. From Settings inside the app, tap Export Data to generate a JSON file of everything — income, bills, expenses, goals, the whole history. You can share it via AirDrop, Mail, Files, or any other iOS share extension. It's your data; take it wherever you want.

My data isn't syncing between devices.

First, confirm you're signed into the same iCloud account on both devices (Settings → your Apple Account). Second, make sure iCloud Drive is enabled (Settings → Apple Account → iCloud → iCloud Drive). Third, confirm Left. is toggled on in the iCloud app list. Sync can take a few minutes after major changes, especially on a spotty network — if it's still not working after 10 minutes, email us with a screenshot of your iCloud settings and we'll figure it out together.

Pro features aren't unlocked after my purchase.

Tap Restore Purchase on the Pro upgrade screen. If that doesn't work, make sure you're signed into the Apple Account that made the original purchase (Settings → your Apple Account → Media & Purchases). If you're still stuck, email us with your approximate purchase date and we'll help sort it out.

I found a bug.

Email hello@left-budget.com with a description of what happened, what you expected, and — if possible — a screenshot or screen recording. Bug reports from real users are how the app gets better, and we genuinely appreciate them.

I have a feature idea.

We read every single one. Send it to hello@left-budget.com with a sentence or two of context about how you'd use it. Ideas that fit Left.'s philosophy of "know what's left, nothing more" get prioritized. Ideas that would turn Left. into a different app get a respectful "not a fit" — but they still get read.

Still stuck?

Email hello@left-budget.com. A real person — not a ticketing system, not a chatbot — will read it and reply, usually within a day. Please include what iPhone you're on and what version of iOS you're running. It speeds things up considerably.
