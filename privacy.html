The short version

We can't see your data.

That's not marketing language. It's architecture. Left. has no servers of its own. Everything you enter stays in your personal iCloud account, end to end.

In one sentence: Left. stores your financial data in your own iCloud account, never sends it to a server we operate, collects no analytics, shows no ads, links to no banks, and doesn't require an account to use.

What we store, and where

Every piece of data you enter in Left. — income, expenses, bills, savings goals, receipts, net worth — is saved to your device's local storage and synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.

This means:

  • Your data travels from your iPhone to Apple's iCloud servers, encrypted in transit and at rest, the same way your photos and notes do.
  • It never touches a server we run, because we don't run any.
  • We have no way to log in and see your information. There is no admin panel. There is no database.
  • If you delete the app and later reinstall it, your data restores automatically from iCloud — because it's your data, in your account, not ours.

What we don't do

Most apps in this category share a long list of third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, A/B testing, and marketing attribution. Left. uses none of them. Specifically:

  • No analytics. We don't know how many people use Left., which screens they open, or how long they stay. We don't use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment, or any equivalent.
  • No advertising. Left. has no ads. It has no ad SDKs. Nothing inside the app is an ad.
  • No crash or error reporting. If the app crashes, we only learn about it if you tell us or if Apple's App Store Connect sends us an anonymized crash report after you opt in through iOS settings.
  • No account creation. There is no sign-up flow. There is nothing to log into. We don't have your email address unless you choose to contact us.
  • No bank linking. Left. never connects to your bank, and we have no plans to. You enter income and expenses manually or scan receipts — that's it.
  • No data selling. There is no data to sell. We don't have it.

What Apple sees

Because Left. uses CloudKit for sync, Apple processes your data in transit. This is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and iCloud terms. Apple's role is limited to storing and syncing encrypted data between your devices. Apple does not share your Left. data with us, and we do not have any API or agreement that would let us request it.

If you're curious about exactly what CloudKit does, Apple publishes detailed documentation at apple.com/legal/privacy.

In-app purchases

Left. Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple's App Store. When you purchase Pro, Apple handles the transaction and provides us with a receipt identifier so the app can unlock Pro features on your device. We receive no personal information as part of this process — not your name, not your email, not your payment method. We only learn that a purchase was made.

You can view, restore, and request refunds for Left. Pro through the standard iOS subscription management screen in Settings.

Receipt scanning

When you scan a receipt, the image is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. The image never leaves your iPhone during scanning. The parsed receipt data (merchant, total, date) and the image itself are then saved to your Left. data store and synced to your iCloud account along with everything else.

Contacting us

If you email us through hello@left-budget.com, we'll obviously have your email address for the duration of the conversation. We don't add it to a mailing list. We don't share it. When the conversation is over, it sits in our inbox, and that's the extent of it.

Children

Left. is designed for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We collect no information from anyone, including children, because we collect no information at all.

Changes to this policy

If anything about how Left. handles data ever changes — if we ever add analytics, or accept an integration that changes this story in any way — we will update this page and note the change prominently in the app before the change takes effect. We will not quietly amend this page and hope you don't notice.

Your rights

Because your data lives in your own iCloud account, you already have full control over it. You can:

  • Export your data from Left.'s Settings screen at any time.
  • Delete specific records, or wipe the entire app's data, from within Left.
  • Delete the app from your device and manage or remove the iCloud data through iOS Settings → Apple Account → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Because we don't store your data on our own servers, there is no "request your data" or "delete my account" process we need to provide — the data was never ours to hold in the first place.
